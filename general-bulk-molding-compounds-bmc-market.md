The market for BMC is primarily segmented into polyester-based and vinyl ester-based compounds. Polyester-based BMC offers cost-effectiveness and good corrosion resistance, making it popular for automotive and electrical applications. In contrast, vinyl ester-based BMC provides superior durability and chemical resistance, often preferred for high-performance applications in marine, aerospace, and industrial sectors. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs and specifications.</p></p>
