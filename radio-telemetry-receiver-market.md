<p><p>The radio telemetry receiver market is segmented into single channel and multi-channel types. Single channel receivers are designed to capture signals from one source at a time, making them suitable for straightforward applications where only one data stream is needed. In contrast, multi-channel receivers can simultaneously process multiple signals from various sources, enabling more complex data collection and monitoring. This versatility allows multi-channel systems to cater to diverse industries, such as wildlife tracking and environmental monitoring, requiring extensive data integration.</p></p>
