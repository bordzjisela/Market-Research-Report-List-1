<p><p>The Convertible Shipper Display market consists of two primary types: corrugated and solid board. Corrugated displays are made from fluted paper sandwiched between linerboards, offering strength and flexibility, making them ideal for shipping and retail presentations. Solid board displays, on the other hand, are constructed from a single layer of dense paperboard, providing a more rigid and polished appearance, often preferred for higher-end products. Both types enhance brand visibility and product accessibility in retail environments while facilitating efficient shipping.</p></p>
