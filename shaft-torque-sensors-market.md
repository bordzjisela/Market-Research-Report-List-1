<p><p>The shaft torque sensors market is divided into static and dynamic types. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors are designed to monitor torque on rotating shafts, essential for real-time performance evaluation in automotive and industrial machinery. Both types play vital roles in enhancing precision, safety, and performance across various sectors, including manufacturing and automotive engineering.</p></p>
