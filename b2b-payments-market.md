<p><p>B2B payments can be classified into two main types: cross-boundary and non-cross-boundary markets. Cross-boundary markets involve transactions between businesses located in different countries, necessitating the navigation of foreign exchange rates, regulations, and varying payment systems. Conversely, non-cross-boundary markets consist of transactions occurring within a single country, where businesses engage in more straightforward payment processes, typically subject to uniform regulations and currency. Both markets demand specialized solutions to address their unique challenges and facilitate efficient payment processing.</p></p>
