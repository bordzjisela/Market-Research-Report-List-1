<p><p>The refined rice bran oil market can be segmented based on extraction methods, primarily involving solvent extraction and mechanical squeezing. Solvent extraction utilizes chemical solvents to efficiently extract oil from rice bran, providing higher yields. In contrast, the squeezing method, also known as mechanical pressing, involves physically pressing the rice bran to extract oil, ensuring a more natural and chemical-free product. Both methods cater to varying consumer preferences for oil quality, extraction efficiency, and production costs.</p></p>
