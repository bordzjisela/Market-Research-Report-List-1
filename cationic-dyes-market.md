<p><strong>The Cationic Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolated Type</li><li>Conjugate Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cationic dyes market is characterized by two main types: isolated and conjugate. Isolated type cationic dyes consist of individual dye molecules with a positive charge, allowing for effective bonding with negatively charged substrates, typically used in textiles and paper. In contrast, conjugate type cationic dyes are formed by the combination of cationic dye molecules with other compounds, enhancing their stability and colorfastness. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries, influencing market demand and innovation.</p></p>
