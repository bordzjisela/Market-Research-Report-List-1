<p><strong>The IoT RFID Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID System</li><li>Active RFID System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID systems market comprises two primary types: passive and active RFID systems. Passive RFID systems utilize tags without their own power source; they rely on energy emitted from a reader to transmit data, making them cost-effective and ideal for inventory management. In contrast, active RFID systems are equipped with their own power source, allowing them to transmit signals over longer distances. This capability makes them suitable for tracking high-value assets and real-time location systems in various applications.</p></p>
