<p><strong>The Laboratory Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMV</li><li>AMV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory ventilators market includes two primary types: Controlled Mechanical Ventilation (CMV) and Assist-Controlled Mechanical Ventilation (AMV). CMV provides ventilatory support by delivering preset breaths at fixed volumes or pressures, ensuring consistent ventilation for patients with severe respiratory issues. In contrast, AMV allows patients to initiate breaths, supplementing their efforts with machine-assisted breaths to maintain adequate ventilation. Both types are essential in clinical settings, enhancing patient care by addressing varying respiratory needs.</p></p>
