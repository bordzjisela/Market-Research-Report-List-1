<p><strong>The Floating Thermometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital floating thermometer</li><li>Graduated floating thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floating thermometer market includes two primary types: digital floating thermometers and graduated floating thermometers. Digital floating thermometers provide quick and precise temperature readings using electronic sensors, often featuring easy-to-read displays and added functionalities like memory storage. In contrast, graduated floating thermometers use a traditional glass tube filled with liquid, marked with scale readings, to indicate temperature visually. Both types are popular for monitoring temperatures in aquariums, swimming pools, and outdoor environments, catering to diverse user needs and preferences.</p></p>
