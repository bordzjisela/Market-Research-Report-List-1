<p><p>A knock sensor is a critical engine component used in internal combustion engines to monitor engine vibrations and detect knocking or pinging. This knocking occurs when the fuel-air mixture in the cylinder detonates prematurely, which can lead to engine damage and decreased performance. By sensing these vibrations, the knock sensor relays information to the engine control unit (ECU), allowing for real-time adjustments to ignition timing and fuel injection, ultimately enhancing engine efficiency and durability.</p><p>The Knock Sensor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for fuel-efficient vehicles and stringent emissions regulations.
