<p><ul><li>OFF-ON(2-position)</li><li>OFF-ON-OFF(3-position)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enabling switches are categorized into two main types based on their positions: OFF-ON (2-position) and OFF-ON-OFF (3-position). The OFF-ON switch allows for a simple binary function, turning a device either off or on. In contrast, the OFF-ON-OFF switch offers an additional middle position, providing greater flexibility by allowing the device to be turned off, activated, or temporarily deactivated without switching completely off. This versatility suits various applications, enhancing control and user interaction.</p></p>
