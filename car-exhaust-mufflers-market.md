<p><strong>The Car Exhaust Mufflers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car exhaust mufflers are essential components designed to reduce engine noise and improve exhaust flow. The market features primarily two types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials, such as fiberglass, to diminish noise. They are effective in reducing high-frequency sounds. In contrast, reactive mufflers use a series of chambers and baffles to disrupt sound waves, effectively canceling out noise. Both types cater to different consumer needs for performance, noise levels, and vehicle types.</p></p>
