<p><strong>The Endoscopic Retrieval Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Snares</li><li>Retrieval Forceps</li><li>Retrieval Graspers</li><li>Magnetic Retrievers</li><li>Biliary Stent Retrieval Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endoscopic Retrieval Device market includes various tools designed to extract foreign objects or tissue during endoscopic procedures. Snares are looped devices that encircle and remove lesions or polyps. Retrieval forceps enable precise grasping of tissue samples, while retrieval graspers provide a wider opening for larger objects. Magnetic retrievers utilize magnets to extract metallic items. Biliary stent retrieval devices specifically target the removal of stents from bile ducts, facilitating better management of biliary conditions. Each device is vital for enhancing procedural efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
