<p><p>The oxygen absorbers market includes two primary types: ferrous and organic oxygen absorbers. Ferrous type oxygen absorbers utilize iron powder that reacts with oxygen to lower oxygen levels in packaging, extending shelf life by preventing spoilage and oxidation. On the other hand, organic type oxygen absorbers consist of natural substances that chemically bind with oxygen, often used in food and pharmaceutical packaging. Both types are essential for preserving product quality and enhancing durability across various industries.</p></p>
