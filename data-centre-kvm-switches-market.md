<p><ul><li>Analog Data Centre KVM</li><li>Digital Data Centre KVM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data Centre KVM switches are essential for managing multiple servers from a single console. The market is divided into two main types: Analog Data Centre KVM and Digital Data Centre KVM. Analog KVM switches transmit keyboard, video, and mouse signals through physical cables, offering a straightforward solution for local server management. In contrast, Digital KVM switches utilize IP-based connections, enabling remote access and control over networks, enhancing scalability and flexibility for data center operations.</p></p>
