<p><strong>The Robotic Angiography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT Angiography Systems</li><li>X-ray Angiography Systems</li><li>MR Angiography Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic angiography system market comprises three main types: CT angiography systems, X-ray angiography systems, and MR angiography systems. CT angiography uses computed tomography to provide detailed vascular imaging, making it valuable for diagnosing various conditions. X-ray angiography employs X-ray technology and contrast agents to visualize blood vessels in real-time, often used in interventional procedures. MR angiography leverages magnetic resonance imaging to create high-resolution images of blood vessels, offering a non-invasive alternative for assessing vascular health without radiation exposure.</p></p>
