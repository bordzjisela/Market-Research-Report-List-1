<p><p>Diesel Multiple Units (DMUs) are self-propelled trains equipped with diesel engines, often used for regional or local services where electrification is impractical. The Diesel-Electric Multiple Unit (DEMU) market utilizes diesel engines to generate electricity that powers electric traction motors, offering efficient performance and lower emissions. Conversely, the Diesel-Hydraulic Multiple Unit (DHMU) market employs a diesel engine connected to hydraulic transmission systems, providing good traction and simplicity in design. Both types cater to distinct operational needs and infrastructure conditions.</p></p>
