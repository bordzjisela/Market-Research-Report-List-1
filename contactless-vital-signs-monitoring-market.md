<p><ul><li>Based Optical Sensor</li><li>Based Radar Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contactless vital signs monitoring market encompasses two main types of sensors: optical sensors and radar sensors. Optical sensors utilize light-based technology, such as photoplethysmography, to measure heart rate and blood flow through the skin. In contrast, radar sensors employ electromagnetic waves to detect vital signs by analyzing movement patterns and respiratory rates. Both technologies aim to provide non-invasive, convenient monitoring solutions for health applications, making them ideal for use in clinical settings, telemedicine, and home healthcare environments.</p></p>
