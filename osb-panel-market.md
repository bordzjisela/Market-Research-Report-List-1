<p><p>OSB panels, or Oriented Strand Boards, are categorized into four types based on their intended use and performance characteristics. OSB/1 is suitable for general indoor use, while OSB/2 is designed for load-bearing applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 is suitable for load-bearing applications in humid conditions, making it more versatile. OSB/4 is engineered for heavy load-bearing and high humidity situations, offering superior performance. Each type serves specific construction needs, with varying resistance to moisture and mechanical stresses.</p></p>
