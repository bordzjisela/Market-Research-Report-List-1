<p><ul><li>Gear Pump</li><li>Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic pump market for excavators is primarily categorized into gear pumps and piston pumps. Gear pumps utilize interlocking gears to generate hydraulic pressure, offering a simple design and reliability for lower flow applications. In contrast, piston pumps employ reciprocating pistons to produce high-pressure oil, making them suitable for applications requiring higher efficiency and power. Both types are essential for excavator performance, influencing efficiency, operational costs, and the overall effectiveness of hydraulic systems in various excavation tasks.</p></p>
