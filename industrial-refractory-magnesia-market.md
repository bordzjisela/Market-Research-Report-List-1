<p><p>The industrial refractory magnesia market is primarily segmented into three types. Fused magnesia is produced by melting high-purity magnesite, resulting in a dense, high-strength material ideal for extreme temperatures. Dead burned magnesia is created by heating magnesite to high temperatures, resulting in a stable, low-reactivity product often used in steel-making and refractory applications. Other types include caustic calcined magnesia and magnesium oxide variants utilized for different industrial purposes. Each type caters to specific applications in various high-temperature environments.</p></p>
