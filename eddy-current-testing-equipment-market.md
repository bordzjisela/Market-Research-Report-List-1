Pulsed Eddy Current utilizes short bursts of electromagnetic fields to detect flaws in conductive materials, making it effective for corrosion detection. Eddy Current Array, on the other hand, employs multiple coils to simultaneously inspect a larger area, offering high-resolution data and improved defect characterization.
